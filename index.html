Who am I?

FPGA and Embedded Systems Engineer building reliable hardware from RTL to real-world applications.

I am interested in digital design, communication protocols, motor control, high-speed interfaces, and practical hardware systems that move from simulation into working prototypes. I enjoy solving complex engineering challenges one clock cycle at a time, with a focus on clean RTL, dependable debugging, and hardware that behaves predictably in the lab.

Here's what I'm good at

RTL Design

Designing and debugging FPGA logic using Verilog and VHDL, with attention to timing, synthesis, and hardware behavior.

PCB Design

Creating schematics, PCB layouts, DRC checks, and manufacturable boards for signal processing and embedded applications.

Rapid Prototyping

Building quick, reliable lab prototypes that connect FPGA hardware, embedded interfaces, sensors, and desktop tools.
